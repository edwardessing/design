Make the hiring process more streamlined

How Hireflow works in 5 easy steps

  • 1
    Create your job.

    A unique link is created for you to share. With SEEK, CareerOne, and Indeed integration, manage your entire applicant sourcing process without ever leaving Hireflow.

  • 2
    Applicants click on the link, upload their resume, cover letter and complete a short basic skills test.

    All applicants are organised on a job-by-job basis. Quickly see who has applied and their stage in the hiring process on mobile, tablet, or desktop

  • 3
    Applicants that pass are displayed for you to review.

    Automatically sort through applicants who meet your basic requirements with an easy to create multiple choice basic skills test.

  • 4
    Shortlist and invite for a recorded video interview.

    Applicants can record responses to your questions, then review them when you want. Create, watch, review, and decide with ease.

  • 5
    Contact those that are successful!

    Organise, schedule, and contact applicants all within the application with automatic calendar synchronisation with Outlook and integrated calling functionality on the mobile application.
