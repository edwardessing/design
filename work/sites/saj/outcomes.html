    My greatest fear as a designer is to become stagnant… My work is largely spurred by relevance and modernity. I am always looking for something new. It’s an insatiable drive and sometimes a burden. I don’t find design to be a very still or settled industry. It moves at an untraceable rate. It’s a big responsibility to try and keep up. Maturing as a designer means going beyond your comfortable limits. Having done this, I genuinely feel my work demonstrates maturity and progression.
